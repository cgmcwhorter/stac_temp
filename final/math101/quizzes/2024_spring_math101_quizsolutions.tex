% Quiz 1
\quizsol \textit{True/False}: The integer 131313 is prime. \pspace

\sol The statement is \textit{false}. We know that an integer $N$ is divisible by 3 if and only if the sum of its digits is divisible by 3. We know that $1 + 3 + 1 + 3 + 1 + 3= 12$ is divisible by 3. Therefore, 131313 cannot be prime. In fact, $131313= 3 \cdot 43771= 3 \cdot 7 \cdot 13 \cdot 37$.
