% Problem 1
\problem{10} As accurately as possible, sketch the feasible region given by the following maximization problem:
	\[
	\begin{aligned}
	z&= 4x_1 + 6x_2 \\
	x_1 + x_2&\leq 10 \\
	2x_1 - x_2&\geq 2 \\
	x_1, x_2&\geq 0
	\end{aligned}
	\]
Is this region bounded or unbounded?
